About Us

Founded in 2019, Delta Shipping Agencies (DSA) has rapidly become one of the leading shipping agency services in the Middle East. Backed by a team of professionals with over 20 years of experience in the shipping industry and Qatar market, we bring unparalleled expertise and local knowledge to the table.

Our success stems from being owned and managed by shipping professionals who possess in-depth industry insights and strong market relationships. Delta Shipping has grown to a position where we aim to be the preferred choice for marine-related services in Qatar by delivering competitive, reliable, and customized solutions to both local and international clients.

At Delta Shipping Agencies, we take pride in our stellar reputation and proven track record in shipping and logistics. We excel at blending global standards with local expertise to deliver consistent, high-quality performance for our customers and principals. Our comprehensive range of shipping services caters to the evolving needs of our clients, including Shipping Lines, NVOCC Agencies, Cargo Agency, and 3PLs..
